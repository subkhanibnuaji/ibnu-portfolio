The Challenge

Over 83.99% of Indonesian homes are self-built, but many lack proper permits, don't meet safety standards, and homeowners struggle to access quality materials and skilled workers at fair prices.

üí°

The Solution

A unified digital ecosystem that guides citizens through the entire home-building journey, connecting them with verified professionals, facilitating permits, and ensuring construction quality compliance.

üìà

The Impact

Streamlined access to housing services across Indonesia, improved construction quality standards, and transparent pricing for materials and labor in self-built housing projects.
